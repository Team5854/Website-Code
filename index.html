We Are Team GLITCH:

Glitch, Team 5854, is a student-run rookie FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team operating at The University of North Carolina Asheville (UNCA). Being a new team, and one of the few teams in western North Carolina, we aim to give students enriching experiences in programming, engineering, design, and business administration. In our generation, we are empowering students with important skills focusing in math and science while exposing to them a possible future.
